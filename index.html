About the platform

What is LMS Zanko?

Zanko is the official Learning Management System of Koya University — Faculty of Engineering. The name LMS stands for three pillars that define the platform:

L
Learning

The core of the platform — delivering structured, high-quality education to FENG students at every stage: B.Sc., M.Sc., and Ph.D.

  • Video lectures & recorded sessions
  • Reading materials & lecture slides
  • Interactive quizzes & self-assessments
  • Student–lecturer discussion forums
M
Management

A complete administrative backbone — giving lecturers and faculty staff full control over course delivery, grading, and academic records.

  • Course & module administration
  • Assignment creation & grading tools
  • Attendance & progress tracking
  • Academic calendar & scheduling
S
System

A reliable, secure, and accessible digital infrastructure that connects students, lecturers, and administrators across all FENG departments.

  • Accessible on any device or browser
  • Secure login via KOU credentials
  • Real-time notifications & updates
  • Integrated with Koya University portal
L earning M anagement S ystem
Zanko is maintained by the Zanko-LMS Committee at Koya University — Faculty of Engineering, in cooperation with the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, Kurdistan Region.
Getting started

How to access Zanko

Three simple steps to get started with the Faculty of Engineering LMS platform.

01

Use your KOU account

Log in with your official Koya University student or staff credentials — no separate registration needed.

02

Find your courses

Your enrolled modules appear automatically on your dashboard. Browse materials, lectures, and schedules.

03

Submit & track work

Submit assignments, sit online assessments, and monitor your academic progress and grades in real time.

04

Stay connected

Message your lecturers, receive announcements, and collaborate with classmates — all inside the platform.

Platform capabilities

Everything you need to learn

Zanko brings together all the tools FENG students and academic staff need in one place.

Course materials

Access lecture slides, PDFs, video recordings, and reading lists uploaded by your lecturers.

Assignment submission

Submit coursework digitally before the deadline and get notified when your work is graded.

Schedule & timetable

View your weekly class schedule, exam dates, and important academic calendar events.

Grades & progress

Track your academic results, GPA trends, and module completion status at any time.

Attendance tracking

Lecturers record attendance digitally; students can view their attendance record per module.

Online quizzes & exams

Take timed assessments and quizzes set by lecturers directly within the platform.

Messaging & forums

Ask questions, share ideas, and communicate directly with lecturers and classmates.

Mobile friendly

Zanko works on all devices — desktop, tablet, or phone — no app installation required.
